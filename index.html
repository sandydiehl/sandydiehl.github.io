Research

My research is in moral, social, and political philosophy, with a particular interest in questions and themes that derive from post-Kantian European philosophy. My dissertation is about the role of mutual recognition in morality and love. I am also working on a project about the idea of immanent critique in the critical social theory of the Frankfurt School.
