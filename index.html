About

I am a visiting assistant professor of philosophy at New York University. Before coming to NYU I was a Junior Research Fellow at Christ Church, Oxford. I received a Ph.D. in Philosophy from Harvard University.

Research

My research interests are in ethics and social and political philosophy. I am currently working on a series of papers about the role of mutual recognition in respect and love.
