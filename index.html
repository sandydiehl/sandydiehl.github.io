Sanford Diehl

sanford.diehl at nyu.edu CV

I am an assistant professor of philosophy at New York University. Previously I was a Junior Research Fellow at Christ Church, Oxford. I received a Ph.D. in Philosophy from Harvard University.

I work on ethics, moral psychology, and social and political philosophy. I am currently writing about the role of mutual recognition in respect and love.
